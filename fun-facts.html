    Quickfire Facts!!


  • The Castle originally overlooked the sea
  • The sea has since resided and a town lies bellow the Castle
  • The Castle is protected by four round towers aswell as a gatehouse
  • Harlech castle is surrounded by a clif on two sides, making assault very difficult
  • Harlech Castle's primary use was to help keep English control over Wales
  • The Castle walls are around twelve metres in height
  • The Castle once help up against a seige that lasted seven years during the 15th century
  • The population or Harlech today is roughly 1,450
  • Most people who live in Harlech come from a long family line of people who lived in harlech
  • One of the more creepy facts of Harlech, It is home to a graveyard from the 1800s
